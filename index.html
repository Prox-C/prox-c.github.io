As our college journey comes to an end,

I want you to know this:

All those years, I bore witness to every effort you made.

I saw everything you gave, and I appreciate it more than you could ever know.

Know that I carry a piece of you with me, wherever I may go.

I wish you well in life, in love, and in all that lies ahead.

Thank you.
